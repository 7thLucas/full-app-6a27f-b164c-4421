# Design Guidelines
## Layout
- Single page, full-viewport hero section
- Hero content centered vertically and horizontally
- Generous whitespace

## Typography
- Hero heading: large, bold (e.g. 5xl–7xl), high contrast
- Subtext: medium weight, muted color

## Colors
- Background: white or very light neutral
- Heading: near-black or deep color
- Accent: one clean accent color (e.g. indigo or slate)

## Components
- Hero section with H1 "Hello World"
- Optional short tagline below
- Clean, no sidebar, no navbar complexity
